# Support Visionaire / Pro (planned)

**The engine is free for everyone, forever** — individuals and companies alike —
under the [Apache License 2.0](LICENSE). You never need to pay to use, ship, or
build on it. This page is about *optional* ways to support the project and what
paid extras are planned (the open-core model: free core, paid add-ons).

## If your company relies on it

Visionaire is built and maintained by one developer. If it saves your team real
time, here are ways to keep it healthy — all optional:

- **Sponsor** — back ongoing development (GitHub Sponsors link coming). Sponsors
  get their logo in the README and a say in the roadmap.
- **Priority support** — a support arrangement for teams that want guaranteed
  response times and direct help (planned).

## Pro add-ons (planned, not yet available)

These would be **separate, optional** premium components — the free Apache core
never loses features to them:

- **WordPress companion plugin** — database-verified origin resolution: the exact
  Elementor control, Site-Editor template overrides, stale-CSS detection.
- **Design-image diff** — overlay a Figma/mockup export on the live page and get
  per-region pixel deviation.
- **Hosted / team option** — a managed way to run it across a team.
