## Why pay for this?

One debugging session pays for a year. Visionaire ends the
"change the button → *every* button changed → re-prompt → re-prompt" loop by
telling your AI agent **which rule wins, in which file, on which line, how many
other elements that rule styles, and the scoped selector that touches only the
element you meant** — plus live fix trials, interaction timelines, animation
diagnosis, and pixel-perfect alignment/contrast audits. 22 tools, deterministic,
no AI inside, works with Claude, Copilot, Cursor, and any MCP client.

## FAQ

**Am I "commercial"?**
If the work involving Visionaire is paid — your job, your agency's client sites,
your company's product — yes. Personal blog, side project with no revenue,
coursework, research: no, you're covered by the free noncommercial license.

**I'm a freelancer using it on client WordPress sites.**
That's commercial use — the Freelancer plan ($49/year) covers exactly you.

**We're an agency — does one Team license cover work for all our clients?**
Yes. Licensing is by *who uses the tool* (your developers), not by how many sites
you point it at.

**What happens if I don't renew?**
You stop using the software commercially (or renew). Licenses are term-based.

**Do students / teachers / researchers / charities pay?**
No — you're already free under the PolyForm Noncommercial license.

**Can I try before buying?**
Yes — 30-day commercial evaluation, no registration required.

**Will you enforce this?**
The license is the legal basis for invoicing organizations that use the tool.
Most companies simply comply; that's the model.

**Invoices / VAT?**
Yes — invoices are provided. Tax handling depends on the payment provider
(being finalized).

**Does the noncommercial version differ from the commercial one?**
No. Same code, same features — the difference is purely the license.
